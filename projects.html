  • MicroPedia

    MicroPedia is a mobile Android application designed for microscope slide viewing. The app offers a range of features, including posting, viewing, and sharing slides, as well as engaging quizzes named 'Guesser' that are randomly generated.

    While these functionalities are currently available, our team is actively working on developing the annotation feature.

    For more information, please visit the project website or repository.
